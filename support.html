Popular FAQs

Download the DigiSanchar app, enter your current location and destination, choose your preferred transport option, and confirm your booking. You can track your journey in real-time.

We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, DigiSanchar Wallet, and cash payments. Digital payments are preferred for contactless transactions.

You can cancel your booking through the app up to 30 minutes before departure. Cancellation charges may apply based on the timing and booking type.

Check real-time updates in the app. If there are significant delays, you can reschedule or get a refund. Contact support for assistance with alternate options.

Use the emergency button in the app, contact our 24/7 safety helpline, or report through the 'Safety' section in Support. All reports are handled with highest priority.
