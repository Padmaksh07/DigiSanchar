The DigiSanchar story

Launched with a simple mission: make everyday mobility dependable and delightful by bringing schedules, routes, pricing, and live status into one intuitive experience.

Building on deep integrations with public agencies and operators, our platform empowers commuters, improves fleet utilization, and supports smarter cities.

  • Inter‑state and inter‑city coverage
  • Buses, trains, and shared mobility
  • Real‑time status and crowd levels
  • Safety and compliance by design
