DigiSanchar – Real-Time Public Transport Platform

DigiSanchar is a smart and user-friendly public transport platform built to make traveling easier, faster, and more connected. The idea behind this project is simple — people should be able to access all public transport services from one place without confusion or delays.

Whether it’s tracking buses in real-time, planning journeys, checking fares, scheduling future trips, or reporting issues, DigiSanchar brings everything together into a single modern platform. The project mainly focuses on improving transportation systems in smaller cities across India, where public transport information is often scattered and difficult to access.

Features
Real-Time Transport Tracking

Users can track buses, trains, and metro services live and receive real-time updates about routes and schedules. The platform also helps users quickly find nearby transport options.

Smart Journey Planner

DigiSanchar allows users to plan trips smoothly by entering their current location and destination. It supports GPS-based location detection, route swapping, and multiple travel preferences such as:

Leave Now
Depart At
Arrive By
Different Travel Categories

The platform supports:

Inter-State Travel
Inter-City Travel
Future Trip Scheduling
Travel History Management
Trip Scheduling

Users can schedule trips in advance, set reminders, and receive notifications before travel.

E-Ticket & Digi-Pass

The system supports digital ticketing and smart travel passes while also helping users compare route prices and fares.

Transport Service Registration

Transport operators can register their services on the platform by adding:

Vehicles
Routes
Schedules
Pricing
Business documents

This helps operators reach more passengers and manage services more efficiently.

Ratings & Reviews

Passengers can share their travel experiences by rating trips, uploading photos, and giving feedback to improve transport quality.

Issue Reporting System

Users can easily report:

Service delays
Safety concerns
Driver or staff behavior
Payment-related problems
Technical/app issues
Location-Based Services

DigiSanchar can detect the user’s current location and help them:

Find nearby bus stops
Locate nearby transport
Get smarter route suggestions
Seat Reservation

The platform also supports seat reservation with fare breakdowns and seat preferences, making the booking experience more convenient.

Objective

The main goal of DigiSanchar is to simplify public transportation by bringing different transport-related services into one centralized platform. The project aims to improve accessibility, safety, efficiency, and overall travel experience, especially for people living in smaller cities.
